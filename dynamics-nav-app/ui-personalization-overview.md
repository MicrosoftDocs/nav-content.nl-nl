##  <span data-ttu-id="02645-161"><a name="PersonalizationWinWeb"></a>Werken met personalisatie tussen Windows- en webclient van Dynamics NAV</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="02645-161"><a name="PersonalizationWinWeb"></a>Working with personalization between the Dynamics NAV Windows and Web client</span></span>
